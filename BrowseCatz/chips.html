Chips

Meet Chips, the adventurous 1-year-old feline with a penchant for exploration. With his sleek black fur and bright green eyes, Chips is always ready for a new adventure. He loves to climb, jump, and play, making him the perfect companion for active households.

Chips is also incredibly intelligent and enjoys learning new tricks and commands. He's eager to please and responds well to positive reinforcement. Chips would thrive in a home where he can receive plenty of mental stimulation and playtime.

Personality

Chips is outgoing and affectionate, always eager to greet new friends with a friendly meow and a gentle head bump. He loves being the center of attention and will happily curl up in your lap for cuddle sessions after a long day of play.
